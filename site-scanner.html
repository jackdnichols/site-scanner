Site Scanner

Four scanners you can point at any site: lower-environment link leaks, missing images, spelling/typos, and free-text word search.

Works on any http(s) site. Each scan stays on the origin of its own Start URL.
Runs as its own extension tab, not injected into the page, so requests to the target site are cross-site fetches. Cookies marked SameSite=Lax/Strict on the target site (the default for most session cookies) will not be sent, so scans see the same content a logged-out visitor would even if you're signed in on another tab.
